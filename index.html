Parasite

The movie Parasite is a 2019 South Korean black comedy thriller directed by Bong Joon-ho. The film foucuses on greed and class discrimination from a relationship between two families, where a poor family who as a whole try to decieve there way into a wealthy household for thier own benefits revealing thier parasitic tendencies as implied by the title.
